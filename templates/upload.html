KCPL Property Tax Estimator

This app was created to enable the Kansas City Public Library to estimate their tax income from property taxes. Users can upload a CSV file containing up to 2000 property IDs. The column name must be parcel_number.

How to Use

Upload your CSV file with the list of property IDs to get a downloadable property information file.

Calculate Tax Revenue

The Tax Revenue Calculator applies a levy rate to a taxable value total As the following: (Taxable Value/100)*Levy Rate. Please specify your levy rate to calculate your estimated revenue. in 2023, the Kansas City Public Library had a levy rate of 0.4977. You can lookup your levy rate at the county website.
